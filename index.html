Bio

My name is Zhi-Hao Lin (林志皓). I am a second year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at UIUC, advised by prof. Shenlong Wang. Previously, I obtained my Bachelor's and Master's degree from National Taiwan University, and was fortunate to be advised by prof. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang. My research interest lies in 3D vision and neural rendering, and the long-term goal is to enable machines to perceive and reconstruct the 3D world accurately and efficiently.
