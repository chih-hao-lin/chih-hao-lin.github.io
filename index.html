Hi! I'm Chih-Hao Lin (æž—å¿—çš“), a third year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at UIUC, where I'm advised by Shenlong Wang. I have the privilege of collaborating closely with Wei-Chiu Ma, Jia-Bin Huang, and David Forsyth. Before joining UIUC, I earned my Bachelor's and Master's degree from National Taiwan University, where I worked with Yu-Chiang Frank Wang.
