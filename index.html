Bio

My name is Zhi-Hao Lin (林志皓). I am an incoming Ph.D. student in Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, and my advisor is prof. Shenlong Wang. Previously, I obtained my Bachelor's and Master's degree in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University, and was fortunate to be advised by prof. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang. My research interest lies in 3D computer vision, deep learning, and neural rendering. My goal is to enable machines to perceive and reconstruct the 3D world accurately and efficiently.
